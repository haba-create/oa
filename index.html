About

Hello, I'm Obenewa

Originally from New York and now based in London, I’m a marketing professional with a background in Psychology and Marketing. Studying both gave me a deep understanding of consumer behaviour, and it’s that foundation that shapes how I approach everything I do today.

I’m equally comfortable analysing data with SPSS as I am crafting brand narratives or building AI agents that automate complex marketing workflows. I believe the strongest work sits at the intersection of creativity and evidence. Human insight paired with real data, strategy informed by how people actually think and feel.
